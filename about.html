            What does Zaka stands for?

            Zaka is a trust based lending institution that is aimed at creating a generation of investors as well as creating credible businesses not forgetting other centered business models. Entreprenuers & those who seek funding are not limited to being invested on, they can also be investors & invest/fund other existing businesses & projects on Zaka

            Our institution helps entreprenuers & those seeking investments build a matrix of investors who invest in them to grow their businesses or projects the introduction of Technology, World is becoming super powerful not only in Gadgets, Technology but also in finances. Such options are not only making high end professionals successful but also giving an average person to grow

            If you are looking for a career with varied opportunities, the chance to make a difference, and where strong social values are shared and rewarded, we has much more to offer. We are never hiding anything it's a crystal clear fact before you.

            Zaka.co.ke is an innovative model that links individual micro-investors directly to the SME’s in need of financing through a web-based platform. This robust Peer-to-Business platform will reduce the cost of resources mobilization significantly ensuring affordable credit to SMEs and a decent return to both social and impact-investors. Zaka.co.ke will be created to facilitate interaction between social and impact-investors with SMEs. The web-portal will hold summarized business profiles of registered SME’s and their pending deals requiring financing.

            Our Vision
